Our Message

The mission of the UNCC MSA is to provide an environment that empowers all Muslim students in UNC Charlotte spiritually, academically, socially, and professionally by building a bridge between the Muslim community at UNCC and the MSA. ⛏

We will achieve this by, increasing Islamic awareness in the society at large, providing spiritual events, educational opportunities, and social and fun programs that make the students' experiences at UNCC beyond successful.

- UNCC MSA Board
