Website Evaluations

Worst Website

This Website does not follow the principles of C.R.A.P.

  • C - Constrast: The elements of the site is all over the place. We have random things Highlighted and the contrast of white and random colored text does not help with visiability
  • R - Repetition: The Website has around 50 items on sale with adds mixed all mushed together which makes it hard to read.
  • A - Alignment: This sites has not Alignment. Everything is mix mashed resulting in elements to be overlaping and chaotic.
  • P - Proximity: There are so many items for sale in very close promixy where it is hard to tell which price/item belongs to which image.

Good Website

This is a Good example of C.R.A.P.

  • C - Constrast: The contrast for this website has a simple white background with a Pink and blue theme with some darker gradients. It is full of animation and allows certain things to popup
  • R - Repetition: While there are some Repetition, it is kept to a minimal
  • A - Alignment: The Alignment uses a lot of sections that prompts the user to scroll down the page.
  • P - Proximity: As you scroll down the page, it gets into some products they sell which fits the overall theme of the website. The top menu is simple condensed down to 2 buttons on the left (Home and Shop All) and thing related to checkout on the right.
